# Data-Analysis-Portfolio

Welcome to my data analysis portfolio! This repository is a collection of my hands-on data projects, where I work through raw datasets to find patterns, calculate KPIs, and turn findings into insights a business could actually act on.
 
I'm an entry-level data analyst building toward job-ready proficiency, with a background in Agriculture and prior experience in roles that demanded precision, documentation, and clear reporting. Each project here reflects real practice, not just theory.

* **Data Extraction & Manipulation:** SQL (joins, aggregations, subqueries), Excel (Power Query)
* **Data Visualization & BI:** Power BI (DAX, data modeling, dashboard design), Excel (Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP)
* **Analysis & Frameworks:** Statistical reporting, KPI development, churn & segmentation analysis, RICE prioritization, SWOT analysis, Value Proposition Canvas
* **Currently Learning:** SQL for analytics (4-week structured self-study, applied to an e-commerce practice dataset)
## 📂 Projects
 
### 1. Customer Churn Analysis — Power BI
Analyzed 10,000 bank customer records to identify key drivers of churn. Built an interactive dashboard with drill-down filtering across 3 European countries — found that customers aged 51-60 in Germany churned at over 50%, well above every other segment.
 
### 2. FMCG Business Analytics Challenge — SkillAhead Data Analytics Challenge 2026
Analyzed 15,000 transactions across 3 years of sales data for GlowMart Nigeria Ltd, identifying key reasons behind a drop in sales. Built a 3-page dashboard tracking 12 KPIs across revenue, customer behavior, and brand performance, and highlighted 3 data-backed recommendations.

### 3. Laptop Sales Dashboard — Power BI
Built a dashboard tracking laptop sales across 3 branches over 2 years, covering $605K in total revenue and 712 units sold. Visualized revenue and unit trends by branch, sales agent, and product to spot where performance was strongest.
 
### 4. Hospital Admission Analysis Dashboard — Power BI
Analyzed 10,000 patient admission records across 2 diagnosis groups, breaking down patient volume by age group and gender to surface admission patterns over time.
 
### 5. Beryl Bank Customer Insights Dashboard — Power BI
Analyzed 1,025 bank customers across 5 branches over 3 years, tracking a churn rate of 33.4%. Broke down active vs. inactive customers by branch, manager, occupation, and age group to identify where attrition was concentrated.
 
### 6. UEFA Champions League Historical Finals Analysis — Excel
Built a pivot-table analysis of every UCL final from 1956 to 2024, covering match results, attendance, and goals scored. Found RealMadrid holds the most titles (15), while Juventus has appeared as runner-up more than any other club (7 times).

### 7. NYC Vehicle Collision Analysis — Excel
Analyzed 238,000+ NYC traffic collision records from 2021–2023, breaking down incidents by borough, time of day, and contributing factor. Found Brooklyn accounted for the highest share of collisions, and driver inattention/distraction was the leading identifiable cause.
